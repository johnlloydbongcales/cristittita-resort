Cristitita Resort

Cristitita Beach in Cantilan, SDS, is a slice of tropical heaven. With soft white sands and clear blue waters, it's a perfect spot to kick back and relax. Palm trees sway in the breeze, and the gentle waves create a soothing soundtrack. As the sun sets, the sky transforms into a canvas of warm colors, making it an ideal place for a romantic evening stroll. Whether you're into snorkeling or just want to soak up the sun, Matho Beach offers a simple yet beautiful escape from the everyday hustle.

about us

Cristitita Beach Resort is located at Brgy. 123, Cantilan, SDS. Offering cozy cottages right by the beach, visitors can enjoy the convenience of staying in comfortable accommodations with easy access to the sun, sea, and sand. The beachside cottages provide a tropical charm, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the natural surroundings. When it comes to dining, the resort boasts a variety of culinary delights. From local seafood delicacies to local cousine, the beachfront cafes and restaurants offer a diverse menu. Whether you're craving a refreshing tropical drink or a hearty meal, you can savor a range of food and beverages while taking in the breathtaking views of the ocean. With its inviting accommodations and a tempting array of culinary options, Cristitita Beach Resort ensures that your coastal getaway is as comfortable as it is memorable.
